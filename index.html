Eden West is a collaborative pianist and music educator based in Southeast Michigan. She completed bachelor’s degrees at the University of Michigan studying piano and music education with Logan Skelton and Ana María Otamendi.

She works extensively with voice and instrumental studios supporting lessons, studio classes, recitals, competitions, opera choruses, and performances.

Recent engagements include the Music Academy of the West and the Collaborative Piano Institute.

Honors include the Presser Undergraduate Scholar Award and the Virginia Allison Collaborative Award.
