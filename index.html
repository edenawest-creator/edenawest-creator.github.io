Biography

Partnership,
Precision,
Presence.

Work Together

Eden West is a collaborative pianist and music educator based in Southeast Michigan. She completed bachelor’s degrees at the University of Michigan, with studies in piano and music education and mentorship from Logan Skelton and Ana María Otamendi.

Eden works extensively with voice and instrumental studios—supporting lessons, studio classes, opera choruses, masterclasses, recitals, and performance preparation. She has also served as a répétiteur for opera productions.

Recent engagements include programs such as the Music Academy of the West and the Collaborative Piano Institute. Eden has worked as a pianist for organizations and events including dance institutes and festivals, youth and community ensembles, and regional competitions.

Honors include the Presser Undergraduate Scholar Award and the Virginia Allison Collaborative Award.
