Eden West

Collaborative Pianist

I am a collaborative pianist based in Southeast Michigan, specializing in vocal and instrumental partnership.

Biography

Eden West is a collaborative pianist and Kovner Fellow at The Juilliard School (starting Fall 2026). A graduate of the University of Michigan, she holds degrees in Piano Performance and Music Education. Her work spans opera coaching, recital collaboration, and educational leadership.
