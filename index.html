Biography

About me, recent work.

Eden West is a collaborative pianist based in southeast Michigan. She holds degrees in Piano Performance and Music Education from the University of Michigan School of Music, Theatre & Dance, where she studied with Logan Skelton and Ana María Otamendi.

During her undergraduate studies, she was recognized as a nine-term James B. Angell Scholar and was awarded the 2024–25 Presser Undergraduate Scholar Award and the Virginia Allison Collaborative Award.

At the University of Michigan, West works as a pianist for the voice department, accompanying lessons, recitals, opera chorus rehearsals, masterclasses, and has music directed opera workshop scenes. Recent professional engagements include the Opera Company of Middlebury in Middlebury, VT, assistant music directing at the Bay View Music Festival, No Fly Productions in Ann Arbor, MI. Additional training and work experience include Music Academy of the West and the Collaborative Piano Institute.
